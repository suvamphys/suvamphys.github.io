Research Expertise

My research expertise is deeply rooted in non-equilibrium statistical physics, with a heavy focus on the intricate dynamics of complex systems. I actively investigate stochastic resetting and first-passage processes to uncover universal criteria, fluctuations, and optimal outcomes in transport phenomena.

Additionally, a significant portion of my work explores synchronization dynamics. Through the lens of the Kuramoto model and swarmalators, my research aims to decode complex rhythms, limit cycle switching, and the directional synchrony of self-propelled, interacting particles.
